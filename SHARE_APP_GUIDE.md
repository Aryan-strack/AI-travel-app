# 📱 How to Share Your Smart Travel Planner App

## 🚀 **Method 1: Expo Go App (Recommended for Testing)**

### For You (Developer):
1. **Start the development server:**
   ```bash
   npx expo start --tunnel
   ```
2. **Share the QR code or URL** that appears in the terminal
3. **The URL will look like:** `exp://192.168.1.1:19000`

### For Your Friend:
1. **Install Expo Go** from Google Play Store or App Store
2. **Scan the QR code** or **visit the URL** in Expo Go
3. **Your app will load instantly!**

---

## 🌐 **Method 2: Expo Snack (Online Sharing)**

### Steps:
1. **Go to:** https://snack.expo.dev/
2. **Upload your project** or paste your code
3. **Share the Snack link** with your friend
4. **Your friend can run it directly** in their browser or Expo Go

---

## 📦 **Method 3: APK File (Direct Installation)**

### For You:
1. **Use the exported files** in `apk-build/` folder
2. **Upload to Android Studio** or online APK builder
3. **Generate APK file**
4. **Share the APK file** with your friend

### For Your Friend:
1. **Download the APK file**
2. **Enable "Install from Unknown Sources"** in Android settings
3. **Install the APK** directly

---

## 🔗 **Quick Share Links:**

- **Expo Snack:** https://snack.expo.dev/
- **Expo Go App:** Search "Expo Go" in Play Store/App Store
- **Your Build Files:** `smart-travel-planner-build.zip`

---

## 📋 **What Your Friend Needs:**

1. **Expo Go app** (for Method 1)
2. **Android device** (for APK installation)
3. **Internet connection** (for all methods)

---

## 🎯 **Recommended Approach:**

**Start with Method 1** (Expo Go) for quick testing, then use Method 3 (APK) for permanent installation.
